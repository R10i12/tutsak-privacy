TUTSAK — Health & Safety Warning

Last updated: February 2026

Important: Please read this information before playing TUTSAK.

Photosensitivity / Epilepsy Warning

TUTSAK contains flashing lights, sudden screen transitions, and rapid visual effects that may trigger seizures in people with photosensitive epilepsy or other photosensitive conditions.

If you or anyone in your family has a history of epilepsy or seizures, consult a doctor before playing. Stop playing immediately and consult a doctor if you experience any of the following symptoms: dizziness, altered vision, eye or muscle twitching, involuntary movements, loss of awareness, disorientation, or convulsions.

Camera Feature Warning

TUTSAK includes an optional blink detection feature that uses your device's camera. When using this feature:

Ensure adequate lighting in your room to prevent eye strain. Take regular breaks every 30 minutes. If you experience eye discomfort, dryness, or fatigue, disable the feature and rest your eyes. The camera feature may not function properly in very low light conditions.

General Health Recommendations

Take a 10-15 minute break for every hour of gameplay. Play in a well-lit room and sit at a comfortable distance from your screen. If you feel dizzy, nauseous, or experience headaches, stop playing and rest. Keep the volume at a moderate level, especially when using headphones.

Horror Content Notice

TUTSAK is a horror game that contains intense scenes, loud sudden sounds, and disturbing imagery. This game is not recommended for players who are sensitive to horror content or jump scares.
