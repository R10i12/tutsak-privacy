TUTSAK — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

TUTSAK is a multiplayer horror game. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the game handles your data.

In short: TUTSAK does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No images, video, or biometric data are stored, transmitted, or shared with any third party.

Camera Feature (Optional)

TUTSAK includes an optional "Blink Detection" feature that uses your device's camera to detect eye blinks for gameplay purposes.

How it works:

The camera feed is processed entirely on your local device. The only data produced is a simple true/false value indicating whether your eyes are open or closed. This value is used solely to control in-game mechanics.

What we do NOT do:

We do not record, store, or save any images or video from your camera. We do not transmit any camera data to any server, cloud service, or third party. We do not use facial recognition or store any biometric data. We do not use the camera for any purpose other than blink detection gameplay.

Your control:

The blink detection feature is disabled by default. You must manually enable it in the game settings. You can disable it at any time. When disabled or when the game is closed, camera access is completely terminated.

Multiplayer

TUTSAK uses Photon Networking for multiplayer functionality. When playing online, standard networking data (such as player position and game state) is transmitted between players through Photon's servers. Please refer to Photon's Privacy Policy for details on their data handling practices.

Data Collection

TUTSAK itself does not collect any personal information, analytics, telemetry, or usage data. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other tracking technology.

Third-Party Services

The game is distributed through Steam. Please refer to Steam's Privacy Policy for information about data collected by the Steam platform.

Children's Privacy

TUTSAK is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
